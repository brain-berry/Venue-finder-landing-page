Frequently Asked Questions

Is the app free to use?

Yes! Venue Finder is completely free for users browsing venues.

Can I list my own venue?

Absolutely. Just sign up and go to the "List a Venue" section inside the app.

How do I contact a venue owner?

Each venue profile includes a contact button to call, email, or chat with the owner directly.

Do I need to create an account?

Creating an account is optional for browsing, but required if you want to book or list venues.

What cities are currently supported?

We currently support Accra, Kumasi, Lagos, and Abuja — more cities coming soon!

Is there a cancellation policy?

Yes. Each venue lists its own cancellation terms. Be sure to check them before booking.

Can I get a receipt or invoice?

Yes, once your booking is confirmed, a receipt will be sent to your registered email.
